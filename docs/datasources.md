Sample Project Ideas:

1. Fraud Detection in Banking Transactions

Dataset: Credit Card Fraud Detection - https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mlg-ulb/creditcardfraud
Goal: Detect fraudulent transactions using machine learning.
Potential Features:
Train a predictive model to identify fraudulent transactions.
Perform feature engineering to improve fraud detection accuracy.
Create an interactive dashboard to visualise fraud trends over time.
Implement real-time fraud alerts using anomaly detection.
2. Sentiment Analysis of Product Reviews

Dataset: Amazon Fine Food Reviews - https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/snap/amazon-fine-food-reviews
Goal: Analyse product reviews to determine sentiment and identify trends.
Potential Features:
Use natural language processing (NLP) to classify reviews as positive, neutral, or negative.
Identify keywords that impact product ratings.
Create a dashboard that visualises customer sentiments over time.
Predict product success based on review trends.
3. Air Quality Prediction and Analysis

Dataset: Air Quality Data -
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sogun3/uspollution
Goal: Predict air pollution levels and analyse key contributing factors.
Potential Features:
Develop a time-series model to forecast pollution levels.
Visualise pollution patterns geographically.
Identify seasonal trends affecting air quality.
Build an interactive dashboard for real-time monitoring.
4. Predicting Employee Attrition

Dataset: HR Employee Attrition - https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/pavansubhasht/ibm-hr-analytics-attrition-dataset
Goal: Identify factors influencing employee retention and churn.
Potential Features:
Predict which employees are at risk of leaving.
Identify key factors (e.g., salary, work-life balance, promotions) affecting churn.
Design an HR dashboard for tracking employee satisfaction.
Suggest strategies to improve employee retention.

Goal: Analyse electricity usage and predict future consumption trends.
Potential Features:
Create an interactive dashboard tracking energy use by region.

6. Traffic Accident Analysis and Prediction

Dataset: US Traffic Accidents -
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sobhanmoosavi/us-accidents
Goal: Identify accident-prone areas and predict high-risk periods.
Potential Features:
Analyse accidents caused by location, time, and weather conditions.
Predict accident likelihood based on historical patterns.
Develop a real-time dashboard for authorities to monitor traffic safety.
Recommend strategies for accident reduction.
7. Movie Revenue and Success Prediction

Dataset: TMDB Box Office Data - https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/tmdb/tmdb-movie-metadata
Goal: Analyse factors influencing movie success and predict box office revenue.
Potential Features:
Identify correlations between budget, cast, and box office revenue.

Create an interactive dashboard showing trends in movie success.

8. Food Price Inflation Analysis 2024

Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/code/sonialikhan/food-price-inflation-2024-import-and-eda
Goal: Analyse food price inflation trends for 2024 and explore key influencing factors.
Potential Features:
Data Import & Cleaning: Preprocess global food price data.
EDA & Trend Analysis: Identify inflation patterns and key drivers.
Time Series Forecasting: Predict future food price trends.
Geographic Insights: Compare inflation rates across regions.
Economic Impact: Assess external factors like climate and trade policies.
Interactive Dashboard: Visualise trends with filters and charts.
Actionable Insights: Provide recommendations for businesses and policymakers.
